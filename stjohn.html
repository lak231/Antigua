Some Mill

The magnificent sugar mill on this plantation stood water-side on the northwestern tip of Antigua, where the Caribbean and the Atlantic shake hands. A rock formation known as \"Boone's Chair\" had been carved out of the rock by early settlers, enabling local Carib Indians to sit and look at the intersection of the two seas.
